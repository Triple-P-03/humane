Our Work

What We Do

Our approach is modeled around bringing change at all levels — from individual families to government policy.

Our Approach

We work with parents, teachers, communities, and governments to create an ecosystem where children are the nation's priority.

Family

Changing behaviors, attitudes & practices towards children.

Community

Mobilizing stakeholders like teachers and village committees.

Public

Sensitizing society to prioritize children's welfare.

Government

Advocating for policy changes at district and state levels.
