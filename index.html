Who We Are?

Humane Foundation is an Organization which is registered by Government of Maharashtra, India.

Our main goal is to offer support to underprivileged people like Orphans, Youth and Children of slum areas. We work directly with local schools and people in rural areas like Nagpur and Yavatmal District.

Service for humanity
01

Digital Literacy

We teach children in rural areas how to use computers, and even train their teachers to upgrade their technical knowledge.

02

Hunger Relief

Our first mission is to provide leftover food from hotels and restaurants to the homeless and hungry people.

03

Development

Focusing on tree plantation, theater workshops, and helping women become more independent and aware of their rights.
